First Play as Warden

  • Left click (press on mobile) any cell to flip it.
  • Or set all cells values from menu.
  • Finally to hide key in it right click on cell (hold cell on mobile).

Now play as 1st prison.

  • Left click (press on mobile) any cell to flip it.

Cell colors

  • Green: Contain Key
  • Light blue: Flipped
  • Gold: Contain Key & Flipped
